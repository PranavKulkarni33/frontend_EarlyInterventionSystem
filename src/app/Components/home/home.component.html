Early Intervention System

Empowering instructors. Supporting students. Predicting success.

eClass offers a wealth of student data — but much of it disappears too quickly to act on. The Early Intervention System bridges this gap by capturing, analyzing, and predicting student performance using intelligent, AI-driven insights.

The Early Intervention System is a prototype project developed as part of EECS 4088 – Engineering Capstone Project at York University. Our goal is to harness the rich but fleeting data available in Moodle — York’s Learning Management System — and turn it into actionable insights using machine learning.

This platform allows instructors to:

Upload and analyze course data.

Train predictive models on past student performance.

Receive intelligent alerts about students who may be at academic risk.

Students can:

View their progress.

Receive feedback based on their own input and historical patterns.

Interpret if they need to pull up their socks in the course.
